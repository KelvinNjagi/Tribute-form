Lionel Andrés Messi

"The Greatest Footballer of all time"

Lionel Messi moments that defined Messi as the best Footballer:

  • 2004 - La Liga debut. At just 17 years, three months, and 22 days old, Lionel Messi makes his official league debut for Barcelona.
  • 2005 - Messi makes his debut for Argentina in a friendly against Hungary at the age of 18.
  • 2005 - Golden Boy -- the award for the best young player in Europe.
  • 2006 - First international goal.
  • 2007 - First club hat-trick.
  • 2009 - Champions League success Messi had already won the Champions League in 2006 when making his way into the first team but he is the star man in 2009. He finishes the campaign as the tournament's top scorer and nets in the final against Manchester United in a 2-0 win at Rome’s Olympic Stadium. He’s won a career total of four European titles to date.
  • 2009 - First Ballon d’Or.
  • 2012 - Barcelona scoring record.
  • 2018 - Captaincy, 10th La Liga title, and a record sixth Golden Boot.
  • 2020 - Record sixth Ballon d'Or.
  • 2021 - Copa América and World Cup triumphs.
  • 2023 - Won the fifa world cup
